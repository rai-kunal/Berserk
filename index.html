About Berserk

Berserk is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Kentaro Miura. Set in a medieval Europe-inspired dark fantasy world, the story centers on the characters of Guts, a lone mercenary, and Griffith, the leader of a mercenary band called the Band of the Hawk.

The series explores themes of isolation, camaraderie, and the question of whether humanity is fundamentally good or evil. Miura's artwork is highly detailed and the storytelling is rich with symbolism and philosophical depth.

First published in 1989, Berserk has spanned over 40 volumes and has influenced countless works in the dark fantasy genre. Despite Miura's tragic passing in 2021, his legacy continues through his magnum opus.

Characters

Guts

The Black Swordsman, a lone warrior marked by the Brand of Sacrifice. Wields the massive Dragonslayer sword.

Griffith

Leader of the Band of the Hawk, whose ambition knows no bounds. Later becomes Femto, a member of the God Hand.

Casca

The only female soldier in the Band of the Hawk. Skilled swordswoman and former love interest of Guts.

Skull Knight

A mysterious entity who opposes the God Hand. Wields the Sword of Actuation and rides a spectral steed.
